Our Story

Creating memories since 2018

Taking your taste buds on a journey of their own.

We use the best and freshest ingredients, and combine them to create the perfect taste for the perfect moment.

Spices of Life

Everyone of us is unique. Each of us comes to an event with different moods and preferences in every moment. In spite of this, we all share commonalities with each other. In the end, we want to make friends and have fun.

MISFITS creates drinks that allows us to enjoy for the commonality of community without sacrificing our uniqueness. We create drinks that allow you to be your own individual in a sea of friends, old and new.

Our Taste

Out of love and respect of our MISFITS, we spend numerous hours reviewing, revising and refining our drinks' recipies. We're also constantly researching for fresh new drinks to share with you. Have a fresh idea for us to try? Submit a request and we will do our best to bring it to life for you, of course, with a little MISFITS magic.

Our Service

What's a quality drink without quality service? Punctuality, speed, quality, hygiene, professionalism and a smile are the words we want lingering as you walk away with your MISFITS' drink. Didn't quite feel that way? Leave us some feedback and we will do our best to impress the next chance we get.
